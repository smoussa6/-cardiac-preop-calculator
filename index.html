📋 Risk Factors for Endometrial Cancer

  • Unopposed estrogen exposure
  • Obesity (increased peripheral aromatization)
  • PCOS/chronic anovulation
  • Tamoxifen use
  • Lynch syndrome (HNPCC)
  • Nulliparity
  • Late menopause
