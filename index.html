📋 AUB-HAS2 Components

  • A - Age ≥75 years (1 pt)
  • U - Underlying heart disease (1 pt)
  • B - Breathlessness/angina symptoms (1 pt)
  • H - Hemoglobin <12 g/dL (1 pt)
  • A - Arterial (vascular) surgery (1 pt)
  • S2 - Surgery is emergency (2 pts)

📋 NSQIP MICA Factors

The NSQIP calculator uses 21 patient predictors including age, sex, functional status, ASA class, emergency status, steroid use, ascites, sepsis, ventilator dependence, cancer, diabetes, hypertension, CHF, dyspnea, smoker status, COPD, dialysis, acute renal failure, BMI, and procedure-specific risk (via CPT code).

Functional Capacity Assessment (METs) ℹ️ Metabolic equivalents (METs) quantify functional capacity. ≥4 METs (ability to climb a flight of stairs) indicates adequate capacity and is associated with lower perioperative cardiac risk.

⚠️ When Functional Capacity is Unknown

If unable to assess functional capacity, proceed based on clinical risk factors (RCRI) and surgery type. Consider stress testing only if it will change management.
